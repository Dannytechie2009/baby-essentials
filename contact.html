Frequently Asked Questions

Our **retail prices** are for single-item purchases by everyday customers. **Wholesale prices** are heavily discounted rates available to registered resellers who buy in bulk (usually a minimum of 6 units per product type). You can register for wholesale via the Register page.

We offer Lagos pickup (Ogba) for free. Shipping within Lagos takes 1-2 business days, and nationwide delivery takes 3-7 business days, depending on your location. Delivery timelines begin after payment confirmation.

We primarily use bank transfers and offer cash payment for Ogba pickups. We are working to integrate secured online card payment options in the near future.
